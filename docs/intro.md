# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This book explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, focusing on embodied intelligence and how digital AI models connect with real-world robotics.

## About This Book

This AI-Native, Spec-Driven Book on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics is designed for advanced students, AI engineers, robotics practitioners, and educators with a background in AI, software engineering, or robotics.

The book follows a layered system architecture approach:
- **Perception** → **Cognition** → **Planning** → **Control** → **Actuation**

Each layer builds upon the previous one, providing a comprehensive understanding of how intelligent systems interact with the physical world.

## What You'll Learn

Throughout this book, you'll explore:

- The fundamentals of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- ROS 2 foundations for humanoid robotics
- Digital twins and physics simulation techniques
- AI perception and learning with NVIDIA Isaac
- Vision-Language-Action pipelines
- Autonomous humanoid capstone architecture
- Hardware and infrastructure considerations

## Technical Approach

This book leverages state-of-the-art technologies:

- **ROS 2**: As the nervous system for robotics applications
- **Gazebo/Unity**: For digital twin creation and simulation
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: For AI processing and sim-to-real transfer
- **VLA Stack**: Vision-Language-Action integration with Whisper + LLM + ROS Actions
